CS Background

I am a UX Designer and Conversion Optimization Specialist with a background in Computer Science. This foundation allows me to understand complex technical systems and collaborate seamlessly with developers.

My journey began in 2021 as a Graphic Designer. Over two years, I developed strong visual design skills, branding knowledge, and an eye for aesthetics while working on various creative projects and marketing visuals.

In early 2024, I transitioned into Landing Page Design. Using platforms like LightFunnels and EasyOrders, I built high-converting pages. Working closely with marketing teams immersed me in Conversion Rate Optimization and Media Buying strategies, teaching me to design for performance and sales, not just looks.

By 2025, I expanded my expertise into full UX/UI Design. I now combine design thinking, user behavior analysis, and marketing insights to create holistic digital products.

My current focus is designing digital products that combine:

  • UX Design

  • E-commerce Strategy
